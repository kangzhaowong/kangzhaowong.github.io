Adele Project
Tags: Mechatronics, Kinematics, Robotic Arm, Raspberry Pi, Electrical, ROS
The aim of this project is to create a cost effective and user friendly robotic arm for food preparing. For this project, I helped to lead the team to solve issues with the previous robotic arm prototype that was done by our seniors, assemble the electrical components of the arm, and wire the electrical components together.
